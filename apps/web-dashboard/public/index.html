Get started in 2 minutes. Control AI access forever.

Configure Your Profile

Tell HMAN what data you want to manage. Add your calendar, contacts, preferences — all via simple text commands.

Your data is encrypted and stored in YOUR vault, not ours.

Configuring via Signal

3

Connect Your AIs

Claude, GPT, Gemini — any AI that supports MCP can connect to your HMAN. They ask permission through you.

LLM-agnostic means you're never locked in.

AI Asks, You Decide

Every time an AI wants your data, you get a message. Reply Y or N. That's it.

You see exactly what they're asking for, every single time.

Your Data, Your Storage

HMAN is a broker, not a vault. Your data lives where YOU choose.

📱

Local Device

Encrypted .hman file stored on your phone or computer.

  • Never leaves your device
  • Backup to iCloud/Google Drive
  • Full offline access
  • Your key, your data
☁️

HMAN Cloud

Zero-knowledge encrypted storage. We can't read it.

  • Sync across devices
  • End-to-end encrypted
  • We never see your data
  • Easy backup & restore

Self-Hosted

Run your own HMAN node on your server.

  • Complete control
  • Your infrastructure
  • Open source
  • For power users
