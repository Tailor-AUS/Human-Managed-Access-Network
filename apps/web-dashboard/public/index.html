How it works

Example: Payment
Origin Energy requests $145.00
Your .HMAN asks:
A) Release credit card to them
B) I'll pay via BPay (5 days)
C) I'll pay via PayID (instant)
You reply: C
✓ Paid. They never got your card.

Your .HMAN asks:
A) Share all meetings
B) Share non-private only

D) Deny

✓ Shared 9 meetings. 3 private hidden.
