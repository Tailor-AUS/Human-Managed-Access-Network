NSSM (Non-Sucking Service Manager)
Version 2.24 (vendored 2014-08-31 release)
Source: https://nssm.cc/release/nssm-2.24.zip

NSSM is released into the public domain by its author Iain Patterson.
The full license statement is reproduced from https://nssm.cc/license:

    NSSM is public domain.  You may unconditionally use it and/or its source
    code for any purpose you wish.  There are no restrictions on its use,
    distribution or modification.

The bundled binary is the upstream `win64/nssm.exe` from the official
distribution zip, copied here unmodified so that
`ops/install-windows-service.ps1` is self-contained and runnable on a
fresh checkout without an extra download step.

If you would prefer to verify and supply your own NSSM build, replace
`ops/nssm/nssm.exe` with the matching binary from https://nssm.cc/ before
running the install script.
