# .HMAN Vision

## The Problem

Every AI wants your data. Every business wants your details. You're constantly copy-pasting, re-entering, and exposing sensitive information.

## The Solution

**One code. One inbox. Full control.**

Your `.HMAN` code is your personal API. Anyone who wants something from you—an AI, a business, a colleague—sends a request to your code. You receive it on Signal. You reply to approve or deny.

## The Principles

### 1. Signal is the Interface
No new app to download. No new account to create. Just Signal—trusted by millions, end-to-end encrypted, open source.

### 2. You Always Decide
Every request requires your approval. No automatic sharing. No background access. You see everything and decide everything.

### 3. Actions, Not Just Data
.HMAN doesn't just share your data—it can act for you. Pay a bill without sharing your credit card. Schedule a meeting without the email back-and-forth. Make a call on your behalf.

### 4. Simple Conversations
No complex interfaces. Just messages:
- "Claude wants your calendar. Y/N?"
- "Origin Energy wants $145. A) Card B) BSB C) PayID"

Reply with a letter. Done.

## The Future

Imagine everyone has a .HMAN code:
- Your boss's .HMAN negotiates meeting times with your .HMAN
- Your doctor's .HMAN requests your health records from your .HMAN
- Your bank's .HMAN notifies your .HMAN of suspicious activity

All conversational. All via Signal. All under your control.

## Why Not an App?

Apps get abandoned. Apps need updates. Apps create friction.

Signal is already on your phone. It already works. It's already secure.

We're building on top of what people already use and trust.

## The Mission

We're not here to make money. We're here to ensure that as AI becomes more capable, humans remain in control of their data and their actions.

Free. Open source. For everyone.
